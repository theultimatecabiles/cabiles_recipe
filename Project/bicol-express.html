Bicol Express

Minutes to make: 46 min

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 pc Ginger
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 250g Pork Belly
  • 2 tbsp Bagoong Alamang
  • 2 cups Coconut Milk
  • 250g Green finger chili
  • 1 Sachet Maggi Magic Sarap

Instructions:

  1. Sauté garlic, ginger, onion, pork and bagoong.
  2. Pour coconut milk and simmer until pork is tender.
  3. Add finger chili and cook for another 10 minutes. Season with MAGGI® Magic Sarap®.
    Transfer into a serving plate and serve.
