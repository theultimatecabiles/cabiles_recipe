Chicken Afritada

Minutes to make: 50 min

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients:

  • 0.25 Vegetable Oil
  • 1 pc Potato
  • 1 pc Carrots
  • 6 cloves Garlic
  • 1pc Onion
  • 0.5kg Chicken
  • 1 pc Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 pc Green Bell Pepper
  • 10 g MAGGI Magic Chicken Cube
  • 0.5 tsp Ground Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 2pcs Bay Leaf
  • 1cup Tomato Sauce
  • 1 cup Water
  • 4pcs Cherry Tomato

Instructions:

  1. Sauté potato and carrot in oil until golden brown. Strain and set aside.
  2. Sauté garlic, onion and tomato in the same pan. Add chicken and sauté for 2 mins.
  3. Add MAGGI® Magic Chicken™️ Cubes, pepper, sugar, bay leaf, tomato sauce and water. Stir and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add bell peppers and sautéed potato and carrot. Simmer for another 5 minutes. Transfer on a serve plate and serve.
