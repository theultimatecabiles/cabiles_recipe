Bicol Express

Minutes to make: 40 min

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients:

  • 0.5 kg Chicken Thigh
  • 0.5 sachet MAGGI® Magic Sarap® 8g
  • 2 tbsp MAGGI® Savor Chilimansi 130ml
  • 2 pcs Egg
  • 0.5 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 0.25 cup Cornstarch
  • 1 cup Vegetable Oil
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 0.25 cup Brown Sugar
  • 0.25 cup Banana Catsup
  • 3 tbsp MAGGI® Savor Chilimansi 130ml
  • 0.25 cup Water
  • 1 stalk Scallion

Instructions:

  1. Season chicken with MAGGI® Magic Sarap® and MAGGI® Savor Chilimansi.
  2. Pour beaten eggs to marinated chicken and mix well. Combine flour and cornstarch. Toss chicken in the flour mixture
  3. Preheat oil. Shake off excess flour and fry over medium high heat until golden brown. Transfer into a cooling rack to drain excess oil.
  4. Sauté garlic in butter in a sauce pan. Add sugar, catsup, MAGGI® Savor Chilimansi and water. Simmer for 1min.
  5. Transfer freshly cooked chicken on a serving plate and top with scallions. Serve with dipping sauce.
