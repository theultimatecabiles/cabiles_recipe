Pinakbet with Pork

Minutes to make: 60 min

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 2 pcs Tomato
  • 1 cup Pork Belly
  • 1 tbsp Bagoong Alamang
  • 1.5 cups Water
  • 1 cups Kalabasa
  • 0.5 cup Okra
  • 0.5 cup Sitaw
  • 0.5 cup Ampalaya
  • 1 cup Eggplant
  • 1 sachet Maggi Magic Sarap 8g

Instructions:

  1. Sauté garlic, onion, tomato, pork and bagoong in oil. Pour water and simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Add kalabasa, okra and sitaw in 2-minute intervals. Add ampalaya and eggplant and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Season with MAGGI® Magic Sarap®. Transfer on a serving plate and serve.
